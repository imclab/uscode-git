   The Secretary of Health and Human Services and the Secretary of Labor shall disseminate to interested persons and groups the changes in this subchapter made by this Act, together with an explanation of such changes, and shall undertake, through appropriate organizations, groups, and coal mine operators, to notify individuals who are likely to have become eligible for benefits by reason of such changes. Individual assistance in preparing and processing claims shall be offered by the Secretary of Health and Human Services and the Secretary of Labor and provided to potential beneficiaries.
