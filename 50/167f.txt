  (b) In the event of disposal under section 167a(a)(4) of this title of any property acquired by eminent domain pursuant to this chapter, the former owner or successor in interest of the rights therein shall have the preferential right to reacquire such property on terms as favorable as those terms whereby disposition may be made under such section.
