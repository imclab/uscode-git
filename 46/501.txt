  (b) T4By Head of AgencyK.–When the head of an agency responsible for the administration of the navigation or vessel-inspection laws considers it necessary in the interest of national defense, the individual, following a determination by the Maritime Administrator, acting in the Administrator“s capacity as Director, National Shipping Authority, of the non-availability of qualified United States flag capacity to meet national defense requirements, may waive compliance with those laws to the extent, in the manner, and on the terms the individual, in consultation with the Administrator, acting in that capacity, prescribes.
  (c) T4Termination of AuthorityK.–The authority granted by this section shall terminate at such time as the Congress by concurrent resolution or the President may designate.
