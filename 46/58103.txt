  (a) T4ProhibitionK.–Except with the written consent of the Secretary of Transportation, a contractor holding a contract under the operating-differential subsidy program or under chapter 575 of this title may not–
    (1) employ another person as the managing or operating agent of the operator; or
    (2) charter a vessel, on which an operating-differential subsidy is to be paid, for operation by another person.
