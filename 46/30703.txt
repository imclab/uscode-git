  (a) T4IssuanceK.–On demand of a shipper, the carrier shall issue a bill of lading or shipping document.
  (b) T4ContentsK.–The bill of lading or shipping document shall include a statement of–
    (1) the marks necessary to identify the goods;
    (2) the number of packages, or the quantity or weight, and whether it is carrier“s or shipper“s weight; and
    (3) the apparent condition of the goods.
  (c) T4Prima Facie Evidence of ReceiptK.–A bill of lading or shipping document issued under this section is prima facie evidence of receipt of the goods described.
