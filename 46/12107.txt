  (a) T4RequirementsK.–A vessel is a wrecked vessel under this chapter if it–
    (1) was wrecked on a coast of the United States or adjacent waters; and
    (2) has undergone repairs in a shipyard in the United States equal to at least 3 times the appraised salvage value of the vessel.
  (b) T4AppraisalsK.–The Secretary may appoint a board of three appraisers to determine whether a vessel satisfies subsection (a)(2). The costs of the appraisal shall be paid by the owner of the vessel.
