  (a) T4In GeneralK.–A person may file with the Federal Maritime Commission a sworn complaint alleging a violation of this part, except section 41307(b)(1).   (b) T4Notice and ResponseK.–The Commission shall provide a copy of the complaint to the person named in the complaint. Within a reasonable time specified by the Commission, the person shall satisfy the complaint or answer it in writing.
