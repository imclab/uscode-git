  (a) T4Licensing RequirementsK.–To be regularly employed in the business of salvaging on the coast of Florida, a vessel and its master each must have a license issued by a judge of the district court of the United States for a judicial district of Florida.
  (b) T4Judicial FindingsK.–Before issuing a license under this section, the judge must be satisfied, when the license is for–
    (1) a vessel, that the vessel is seaworthy and properly equipped for the business of saving property shipwrecked and in distress; or
    (2) a master, that the master is trustworthy and innocent of any fraud or misconduct related to property shipwrecked or saved on the coast.
