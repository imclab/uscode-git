  (a) Purchase of commodities
     From amounts made available to carry out this chapter, for each of fiscal years 2002 through 2007, the Secretary shall purchase $140,000,000 of a variety of nutritious and useful commodities of the types that the Secretary has the authority to acquire through the Commodity Credit Corporation or under section 612c of this title and distribute the commodities to States for distribution in accordance with section 7515 of this title.
