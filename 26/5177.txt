  (a) General provisions relating to bonds
     The provisions of section 5551 shall be applicable to the bonds required by or given under sections 5173 and 5175.
  (b) Cross references
     T2(1) For deposit of United States bonds or notes in lieu of sureties, see section 9303 of title 31, United States Code.
     T2(2) For penalty and forfeiture for failure or refusal to give bond, or for giving false, forged, or fraudulent bond, or carrying on the business of a distiller without giving bond, see sections 5601(a)(4), 5601(a)(5), 5601(b), and 5615(3).
