  (a) T4Affirmative DefenseK.–It is an affirmative defense to a prosecution under any Federal statute that, at the time of the commission of the acts constituting the offense, the defendant, as a result of a severe mental disease or defect, was unable to appreciate the nature and quality or the wrongfulness of his acts. Mental disease or defect does not otherwise constitute a defense.
  (b) T4Burden of ProofK.–The defendant has the burden of proving the defense of insanity by clear and convincing evidence.
