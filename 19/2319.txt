  (b) Any person who commits an offense under subsection (a) of this section–
    (1) shall be imprisoned not more than 5 years, or fined in the amount set forth in this title, or both, if the offense consists of the reproduction or distribution, during any 180-day period, of at last \1\N 10 copies or phonorecords, of 1 or more copyrighted works, with a retail value of more than $2,500;

    (3) shall be imprisoned not more than 1 year, or fined in the amount set forth in this title, or both, in any other case.
