   Codification
   Sections 1231 to 1234 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.
   Section 1231, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 1, 47 Stat. 761; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 1, 48 Stat. 456, related to a convention to frame a constitution for Philippines. Section 1 of act Jan. 17, 1933, was repealed by Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 648.
   Section 1232, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 10, 47 Stat. 768; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 2, 48 Stat. 457; Proc. No. 2695, eff. July 4, 1946, 11 F.R. 7515, 60 Stat. 1352, related to mandatory provisions of constitution.
   Section 1233, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 3, 47 Stat. 763; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 3, 48 Stat. 458, related to submission of proposed constitution to President of United States.
   Section 1234, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 4, 47 Stat. 763; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 4, 48 Stat. 458, related to submission of constitution to Filipino people.
