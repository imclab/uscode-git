   Codification
   Section 120, act July 5, 1932, ch. 421, § 1, 47 Stat. 576, related to equipment necessary on ocean-going vessels using ports in the Canal Zone. See section 361 of Title 2, Canal Zone Code.
   Section 121, act July 5, 1932, ch. 421, § 2, 47 Stat. 576, related to jurisdiction of violations and penalties for ocean-going vessels not properly equipped. See section 362 of Title 2, Canal Zone Code.
   Pub. L. 96–70, title III, § 3303(b), Sept. 27, 1979, 93 Stat. 499, redesignated the Canal Zone Code as the Panama Canal Code. See also section 3602(d) of Title 22, Foreign Relations and Intercourse.
