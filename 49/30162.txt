  (a) T4FilingK.–Any interested person may file a petition with the Secretary of Transportation requesting the Secretary to begin a proceeding–
    (1) to prescribe a motor vehicle safety standard under this chapter; or
    (2) to decide whether to issue an order under section 30118(b) of this title.
  (b) T4Statement of FactsK.–The petition must state facts that the person claims establish that a motor vehicle safety standard or order referred to in subsection (a) of this section is necessary and briefly describe the order the Secretary should issue.
  (c) T4ProceedingsK.–The Secretary may hold a public hearing or conduct an investigation or proceeding to decide whether to grant the petition.
  (d) T4Actions of SecretaryK.–The Secretary shall grant or deny a petition not later than 120 days after the petition is filed. If a petition is granted, the Secretary shall begin the proceeding promptly. If a petition is denied, the Secretary shall publish the reasons for the denial in the Federal Register.
