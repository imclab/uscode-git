  (a) T4AuthorityK.–The Secretary of a military department may order to active duty a retired officer having expertise as an aviator to fill staff positions normally filled by aviators on active duty. Any such order may be made only with the consent of the officer ordered to active duty and in accordance with an agreement between the Secretary and the officer.
  (b) T4DurationK.–The period of active duty of an officer under an order to active duty under subsection (a) shall be specified in the agreement entered into under that subsection.
  (c) T4LimitationK.–No more than a total of 500 officers may be on active duty at any time under subsection (a).
  (d) T4Relationship to Other AuthorityK.–The authority to order a retired officer to active duty under this section is in addition to the authority under section 688 of this title or any other provision of law authorizing the Secretary concerned to order a retired member to active duty.
  (e) T4Inapplicability of Certain ProvisionsK.–Officers ordered to active duty under subsection (a) shall not be counted for purposes of section 688 or 690 of this title.
  (f) T4Expiration of AuthorityK.–An officer may not be ordered to active duty under this section after September 30, 2008.
