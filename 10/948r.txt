  (d) T4Statements Obtained After Enactment of Detainee Treatment Act ofK 2005.–A statement obtained on or after December 30, 2005 (the date of the enactment of the Defense \1\ Treatment Act of 2005) in which the degree of coercion is disputed may be admitted only if the military judge finds that–
