  (a) T4Authorization To ReimburseK.–The Secretary concerned may reimburse a member of the armed forces under the jurisdiction of the Secretary for travel and related expenses (to the extent not otherwise reimbursable under law) incurred by the member as a result of the cancellation of previously approved leave when the leave is canceled in connection with the member“s participation in a contingency operation and the cancellation occurs within 48 hours of the time the leave would have commenced.
  (b) T4RegulationsK.–The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations to establish the criteria for the applicability of subsection (a).
  (c) T4Conclusiveness of SettlementK.–The settlement of an application for reimbursement under subsection (a) is final and conclusive.
