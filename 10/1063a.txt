  (a) T4Eligibility of MembersK.–A member of the National Guard who, although not in Federal service, is called or ordered to duty in response to a federally declared disaster shall be permitted to use commissary stores and MWR retail facilities during the period of such duty on the same basis as members of the armed forces on active duty.
  (b) T4Eligibility of DependentsK.–A dependent of a member of the National Guard who is permitted under subsection (a) to use commissary stores and MWR retail facilities shall be permitted to use such stores and facilities, during the same period as the member, on the same basis as dependents of members of the armed forces on active duty.
  (c) T4DefinitionsK.–In this section:
    (1) T4Federally declared disasterK.–The term ””federally declared disaster““ means a disaster or other situation for which a Presidential declaration of major disaster is issued under section 401 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 5170).
    (2) T4MWR retail facilitiesK.–The term ””MWR retail facilities““ has the meaning given that term in section 1065(e) of this title.
