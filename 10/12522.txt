  (a) T4EstablishmentK.–The Secretary shall establish for members of the Ready Reserve (including the Coast Guard Reserve) an insurance program to be known as the ””Ready Reserve Mobilization Income Insurance Program““.
  (b) T4AdministrationK.–The insurance program shall be administered by the Secretary. The Secretary may prescribe in regulations such rules, procedures, and policies as the Secretary considers necessary or appropriate to carry out the insurance program.
