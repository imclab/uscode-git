  (a) T4NondisclosureK.–The proceedings of a selection board convened under section 611 this title may not be disclosed to any person not a member of the board.
  (b) T4Prohibited Uses of Board Discussions, Deliberations, and RecordsK.–The discussions and deliberations of a selection board described in subsection (a) and any written or documentary record of such discussions and deliberations–
    (1) are immune from legal process;
    (2) may not be admitted as evidence; and
    (3) may not be used for any purpose in any action, suit, or judicial or administrative proceeding without the consent of the Secretary of the military department concerned.
