  (a) T4AuthorityK.–The Secretary of the Navy may provide, by contract or otherwise, necessary salvage facilities for public and private vessels.
  (b) T4Coordination With Secretary of Homeland SecurityK.–The Secretary shall submit to the Secretary of Homeland Security for comment each proposed contract for salvage facilities that affects the interests of the Department of Homeland Security.
  (c) T4LimitationK.–The Secretary of the Navy may enter into a term contract under subsection (a) only if the Secretary determines that available commercial salvage facilities are inadequate to meet the requirements of national defense.
  (d) T4Public NoticeK.–The Secretary may not enter into a contract under subsection (a) until the Secretary has provided public notice of the intent to enter into such a contract.
