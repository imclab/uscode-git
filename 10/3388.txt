   If a commissioned officer of the Army Reserve who enters upon active duty (other than for training) while his name is on a zone of consideration list under section 3383 of this title has had his name submitted to a selection board, or is recommended for promotion under section 3383 or 3384 of this title, his name shall be removed from the list or withdrawn from those recommended for promotion, and he shall be treated as if he had not been considered for promotion.
