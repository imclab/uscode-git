  (a) T4ProhibitionK.–Except as provided in subsection (b), no Army band or member thereof may receive remuneration for furnishing music outside the limits of an Army post in competition with local civilian musicians.  
  (b) T4RecordingsK.–(1) Any Army band designated as a special band may produce recordings for commercial sale.
