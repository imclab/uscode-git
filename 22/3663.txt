  (a) Prescription of regulations
     The President shall prescribe regulations necessary and appropriate to carry out the provisions of this subpart and coordinate the policies and activities of agencies under this subpart.
  (b) Establishment of office; duties
     The President may establish an office within the Commission as the successor to the Canal Zone Central Examining Office. The purpose of the office shall be to assist the President in–
    (1) carrying out the President“s coordination responsibility under subsection (a) of this section; and
    (2) implementing the provisions of the Panama Canal Treaty of 1977 and related agreements with respect to recruitment, examination, determination of qualification standards, and similar matters.
  (c) Delegation by President
     The President may delegate any authority vested in the President by this subpart and may provide for the redelegation of that authority.
