  (b) Extension or renewal of limited appointments of Foreign Service members
     Limited appointments of members of the Foreign Service for the Agency may be extended or renewed, notwithstanding section 309 of the Foreign Service Act of 1980 [22 U.S.C. 3949], so long as the service of the individual under such appointment does not exceed ten consecutive years without a break in service of at least one year.
