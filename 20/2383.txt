  (a) Sharing of information with administrators
     The State board shall ensure that the experience and information gained through programs assisted under this part is shared with administrators for the purpose of program planning. Funds available under this part shall be used to assist in providing State leadership qualified by experience and knowledge in guidance and counseling.
  (b) Limitation on use of funds
     Not more than 6 percent of the funds available under this part may be used for State administration of projects, services, and activities under this part.
