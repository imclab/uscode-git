   It is the purpose of this part to encourage partnerships between institutions of higher education or State higher education agencies and secondary schools serving low-income and disadvantaged students, to support programs that–
  (1) improve the retention and graduation rates at such secondary schools;
  (2) improve the academic skills of public and private nonprofit secondary school students;
  (3) increase such students“ opportunities to continue a program of education after secondary school; and
  (4) improve such students“ prospects for employment after secondary school.
