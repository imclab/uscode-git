  (a) Authority
     The Secretary is authorized to make grants to pay the Federal share of carrying out the purposes of this subpart to a partnership betweenâ€“
    (1) one or more institutions of higher education which have a demonstrated record and special expertise in carrying out the purposes of this subpart; and

      (C) community-based organizations.
  (b) Federal share
     The Federal share of each grant awarded under this section shall be 50 percent.
  (c) Non-Federal share
     The non-Federal share of each grant awarded under this section may be in cash or kind fairly evaluated, including planned equipment or services.
  (d) Administrative costs
     Not more than 5 percent of any grant awarded under this section may be used for administrative expenses.
