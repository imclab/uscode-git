   Sections 1147 to 1150, Pub. L. 89–329, title XII, §§ 1207–1210, as added Pub. L. 90–575, title II, § 295, Oct. 16, 1968, 82 Stat. 1051, 1052, provided for program planning and evaluation for higher education programs; advance funding; evaluation reports and Congressional review; and availability of appropriations on academic or school year basis.
