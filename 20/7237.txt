  (a) Evaluation and dissemination
     The Secretary shall evaluate in accordance with section 8941 of this title, and disseminate, materials and programs developed under this part and shall report to the Congress regarding such evaluation materials and programs not later than January 1, 1998.
  (b) Program operations
     The Secretary shall ensure that the activities assisted under this part are administered within the Department by a person who has recognized professional qualifications and experience in the field of gender equity education.
