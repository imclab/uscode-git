  (a) T4In General.–
    (1) T4AppointmentK.–Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, the conservator or receiver who files the petition shall be the trustee under this chapter, unless the Board designates an alternative trustee.
    (2) T4SuccessorK.–The Board may designate a successor trustee if required.
  (b) T4Authority of TrusteeK.–Whenever the Board appoints or designates a trustee, chapter 3 and sections 704 and 705 of this title shall apply to the Board in the same way and to the same extent that they apply to a United States trustee.
