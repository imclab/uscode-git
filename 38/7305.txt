   The Veterans Health Administration shall include the following:
  (1) The Office of the Under Secretary for Health.
  (2) A Medical Service.
  (3) A Dental Service.
  (4) A Podiatric Service.
  (5) An Optometric Service.
  (6) A Nursing Service.
  (7) Such other professional and auxiliary services as the Secretary may find to be necessary to carry out the functions of the Administration.
