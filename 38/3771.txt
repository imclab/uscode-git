   For purposes of this subchapter:
  (1) The term ””veteran““ has the meaning given such term by paragraph (2) of section 101.
  (2) The term ””homeless veteran““ means a veteran who is a homeless individual.
  (3) The term ””homeless individual““ has the meaning given such term by section 103 of the Stewart B. McKinney Homeless Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 11302).
