   Codification
   Section 147a, acts Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412, title I, § 102, 58 Stat. 735, as amended, which related to fees for inspection of plants for exporting or transiting, was transferred to section 7759 of this title.
   Section 147b, Pub. L. 97–46, § 1, Sept. 25, 1981, 95 Stat. 953, as amended, which related to transfer of funds for emergency arrest of animal or poultry diseases, was transferred to section 129a of Title 21, Food and Drugs.
