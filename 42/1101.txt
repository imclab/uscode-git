  (a) T4OrganizationK.–There is an Office of Federal Procurement Policy in the Office of Management and Budget.
  (b) T4PurposesK.–The purposes of the Office of Federal Procurement Policy are to–
    (1) provide overall direction of Government-wide procurement policies, regulations, procedures, and forms for executive agencies; and
    (2) promote economy, efficiency, and effectiveness in the procurement of property and services by the executive branch of the Federal Government.
  (c) T4Authorization of AppropriationsK.–Necessary amounts may be appropriated each fiscal year for the Office of Federal Procurement Policy to carry out the responsibilities of the Office for that fiscal year.
