  (a) T4DefinitionK.–In this section, the term ””proposal““ means a proposal, including a technical, management, or cost proposal, submitted by a contractor in response to the requirements of a solicitation for a competitive proposal.
  (b) T4ProhibitionK.–A proposal in the possession or control of an executive agency may not be made available to any person under section 552 of title 5.
