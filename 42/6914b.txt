   As used in this title–
  (1) the term ””regulated item““ means any plastic ring carrier device that contains at least one hole greater than 1\3/4\ inches in diameter which is made, used, or designed for the purpose of packaging, transporting, or carrying multipackaged cans or bottles, and which is of a size, shape, design, or type capable, when discarded, of becoming entangled with fish or wildlife; and
  (2) the term ””naturally degradable material““ means a material which, when discarded, will be reduced to environmentally benign subunits under the action of normal environmental forces, such as, among others, biological decomposition, photodegradation, or hydrolysis.
