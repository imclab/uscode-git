  (a) General rule
     All unenhanced land remote sensing data gathered and owned by the United States Government, including unenhanced data gathered under the technology demonstration program carried out pursuant to section 5633 of this title, shall be made available to users in a timely fashion.
  (b) Protection for commercial data distributor
     The President shall seek to ensure that unenhanced data gathered under the technology demonstration program carried out pursuant to section 5633 of this title shall, to the extent practicable, be made available on terms that would not adversely effect \1\N the commercial market for unenhanced data gathered by the Landsat 6 spacecraft.
