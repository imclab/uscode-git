   Subject to the provisions relating to the registration of trade-marks,\1\N so far as they are applicable, service marks shall be registrable, in the same manner and with the same effect as are trade-marks,\1\ and when registered they shall be entitled to the protection provided in this chapter in the case of trade-marks.\1\ Applications and procedure under this section shall conform as nearly as practicable to those prescribed for the registration of trade-marks.\1\
