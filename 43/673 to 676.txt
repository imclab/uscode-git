   Section 673, R.S. § 2354, authorized private sales of all public lands in entire, half, etc., sections.
   Section 674, R.S. § 2355; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, 60 Stat. 1100, required a memorandum in connection with application for purchase of public land at private sale.
   Section 675, act May 18, 1898, ch. 344, § 2, 30 Stat. 418, set forth requirements for private sale of public lands in Missouri.
   Section 676, R.S. § 2365; 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, 60 Stat. 1100, authorized sale of public land to highest bidder at the private sale of land.
