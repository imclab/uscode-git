  (a) T4In GeneralK.–Except as otherwise specifically provided in this chapter, as used in this chapter:
    (1) T4BoardK.–The term ””Board““ means the Merit Systems Protection Board under chapter 12 of title 5.
    (2) T4Covered employeeK.–The term ””covered employee““ means any employee of an employing office.
    (3) T4EmployeeK.–The term ””employee““ includes an applicant for employment and a former employee.
    (4) T4Employing officeK.–The term ””employing office““ means–
      (A) each office, agency, or other component of the Executive Office of the President;

      (C) the official residence (temporary or otherwise) of the Vice President.
  (b) T4Definitions Relating to Certain MattersK.–For purposes of applying this chapter with respect to any practice or other matter–

    (2) to which section 412 relates, the term ””covered employee““ means a covered employee described in section 412(a)(2)(B);
    (3) to which section 413 relates, the term ””covered employee““ excludes interns and volunteers, as described in section 413(a)(2); and
    (4) to which section 416 relates, the term ””covered employee““ means a covered employee described in section 416(a)(2).
