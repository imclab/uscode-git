   As used in this chapter–
  (1) ””Conference““ shall mean the Judicial Conference of the United States;
  (2) ””Council““ shall mean the Judicial Council of the Circuit;
  (3) ””Director““ shall mean the Director of the Administrative Office of the United States Courts;
  (4) ””Full-time magistrate““ shall mean a full-time United States magistrate;
  (5) ””Part-time magistrate““ shall mean a part-time United States magistrate; and
  (6) ””United States magistrate““ and ””magistrate““ shall mean both full-time and part-time United States magistrates.
