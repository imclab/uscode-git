  (a) Short title
     This chapter may be cited as the ””Government Employee Rights Act of 1991““.
  (b) Purpose
     The purpose of this chapter is to provide procedures to protect the rights of certain government \1\N employees, with respect to their public employment, to be free of discrimination on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, or disability.
  (c) ””Violation““ defined
     For purposes of this chapter, the term ””violation““ means a practice that violates section 1202(a) of this title.
