  (a) Amount
     There are authorized to be appropriated to the Fund $50,000,000 to carry out this subchapter.
  (b) Budgetary treatment
     The amount authorized to be appropriated under subsection (a) of this section shall be subject to discretionary spending caps, as provided in section 665 of title 2, and therefore shall reduce by an equal amount funds made available for other discretionary spending programs.
