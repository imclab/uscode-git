   It shall be the duty of all persons employed in the service of the Government in the Capitol or in the United States Capitol Grounds to prevent, as far as may be in their power, offenses against sections 193a to 193m, 207a, 212a, 212a–2, 212a–3, and 212b of this title, and to aid the police, by information or otherwise, in securing the arrest and conviction of offenders.
