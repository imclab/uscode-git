  (a) T4RequirementK.–The Secretary shall establish a uniform system to service the Federal credit instruments made available under this chapter.
  (b) T4Fees.–
    (1) T4In generalK.–The Secretary may collect and spend fees, contingent upon authority being provided in appropriations Acts, at a level that is sufficient to cover–
      (A) the costs of services of expert firms retained pursuant to subsection (d); and
      (B) all or a portion of the costs to the Federal Government of servicing the Federal credit instruments.
  (c) T4Servicer.–
    (1) T4In generalK.–The Secretary may appoint a financial entity to assist the Secretary in servicing the Federal credit instruments.
    (2) T4DutiesK.–The servicer shall act as the agent for the Secretary.
    (3) T4FeeK.–The servicer shall receive a servicing fee, subject to approval by the Secretary.
  (d) T4Assistance From Expert FirmsK.–The Secretary may retain the services of expert firms, including counsel, in the field of municipal and project finance to assist in the underwriting and servicing of Federal credit instruments.
